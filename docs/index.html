FAQ

How do I get the download after paying?
You'll receive a download link immediately after payment via the confirmation page and email receipt.
Which Macs work?
Apple Silicon MacBooks with M1 Pro or newer. These have the built-in accelerometer that SlapSound reads. The base M1, Mac Mini, Mac Studio, and Intel Macs don't have it.
Will it damage my MacBook?
No. The accelerometer detects vibration from a light tap. You don't need to hit hard. A firm tap on the side or trackpad area is enough.
What's Tony Stark Mode?
It replaces all slap sounds with J.A.R.V.I.S. sci-fi beeps. Double-clap or say "Jarvis are you there" to open Terminal with Claude Code and play the Iron Man theme. Voice recognition runs locally.
Can I add my own sounds?
Yes. Record up to 4 seconds from your mic or upload any MP3/WAV file. Your custom sound persists across sessions.
Can I get a refund?

Is it on the App Store?
No. Accelerometer access requires running outside the macOS sandbox. SlapSound is distributed as a signed, Apple-notarized DMG directly from agaro.ai.
